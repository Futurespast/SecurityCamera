About Us


This security app, powered by a Raspberry Pi, is designed to enhance your safety. Equipped with sensors that detect movement and capture faces, it provides a comprehensive solution for monitoring your surroundings. Users can easily view videos, identify unknown faces, and ensure the security of their premises. The videos are fetched from the Raspberry Pi, to the database and are displayed in our website.
